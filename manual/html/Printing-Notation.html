1.3.3 Printing Notation

Many of the examples in this manual print text when they are evaluated. In examples that print text, the printed text is indicated with ‘-|’. The value returned by evaluating the form (here #t) follows on a separate line. 

     ($sequence (write 1) (write 2) #t)
          -| 1
          -| 2
          ⇒ #t
